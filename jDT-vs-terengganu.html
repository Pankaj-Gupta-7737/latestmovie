The rivalry between JDT and Terengganu dates back to the early days of the Malaysia Super League. Both teams have consistently been among the top contenders, making their matches highly competitive. Here are some key moments in their rivalry:

  • 2018 Super League Clash: Terengganu stunned JDT with a 2-1 victory, ending JDT’s unbeaten streak.
  • 2022 FA Cup Semi-Final: JDT emerged victorious in a penalty shootout, advancing to the final.
